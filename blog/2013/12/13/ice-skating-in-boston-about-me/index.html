Ice Skating in Boston

Dec 13th, 2013

November 2013 I joined the winter cohort of Launch Academy in Boston.

I was first introduced to Ruby on Rails at a @railsgirls_bln event in May 2012. End of August 2012 I joined a project group called the @rubymonster hosted by Travis CI. OpenTechSchool Berlin and Rails Girls Berlin events and people have been an integral part of my learnings.

Ever since I am enjoying ice-skating in Boston common or Kendall square.
